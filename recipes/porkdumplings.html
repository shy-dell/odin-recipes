Pork Dumplings

This Chinese dumplings recipe is from Liana Cafe House in Seattle's International District. They sell these traditional fried pork dumplings from a tiny takeout shop but with this recipe, you can see how to make them at home!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Pork
  • Garli
  • Large Egg
  • Chinese Chives
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Minced Ginger
  • Dumpling Wrappers
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps:

  1. Make Dipping Sauce
  2. Mix Dumpling Filling
  3. Fill Wrappers with Filling
  4. Wet Edge of Wrapper and Crimp to Form Dumpling
  5. Fry in Vegetable Oil
  6. Steam with 1 Cup of Water
