Steamed Fish

An easy, fast, and yummy fish dish that my family absolutely loves. This is my mom from China's recipe. Serve with rice.

Ingredients:

  • Red Snapper Filets
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Grated Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Tomato
  • Red Chile Pepper
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Boil Water and Fit Steamer
  2. Add toppings to the Fish
  3. Steam Fish for 15 Minutes
  4. That's it!
