Char Siu

This Chinese barbeque pork is glossy, deliciously savory, and quite easy to make at home â€” even without a fancy ceramic grill.

Ingredients:

  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Rice Wine
  • Hoison
  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Chinese 5 Spice Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Pink Curing Salt
  • Boneless Pork Butt
  • Red Food Coloring
  • Kosher Salt

Steps:

  1. Simmer Sauce in Pan
  2. Cut Pork Lengthwise
  3. Add Food Coloring and Marinate Pork
  4. Preheat Grill to 300F
  5. Remove from Marinade
  6. Grill Pork
  7. Boil Remaining Marinade
  8. Brush Marinade onto Pork
